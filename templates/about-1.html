Our Story

HOW WE FIRST MET

How did our love story begin? April 12, 2019 John and Sarah were on a dating website. They began talking, and while neither were anticipating a relationship, John asked Sarah if she would attend a Christian concert with him. Sarah accepted the offer, as she was excited for a night out. As what seemed as just a casual meet up at a concert, turned into a date of laughs, drinks, food and a walk on the beach. And so, the journey began and John and Sarah became inseparable. John and Sarah went on many adventures together, always having a good time. At the beginning of their dating story, John bought a dinghy to relax and enjoy on the water. John and Sarah spent many evenings and days fishing and being on the water.

EPIC

On one evening, John asked Sarah, along with a couple friends if they would attend a fundraiser called EPIC. John had raved about this event as being one of his favourite nights. So on August 31,, 2019 John took Sarah and friends to enjoy a night of socializing and eating a really good meal to fundraise for a local charity. That night, while John and Sarah were sitting at a table, John looked at Sarah and told her he loved her. This turned out to be one of the best and most memorable nights.
